## Go-to-Market Strategy (First 90 Days)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- Buy domain (sierratechspaces.com or sierratech.eg)
- Build landing page: single-page, Arabic/English toggle, WhatsApp CTA
- Social accounts: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, WhatsApp Business
- 2-3 case study mockups (before/after)
- CRM setup (HubSpot free or Google Sheet)

### Week 3-4: First Clients (Proof of Concept)
- Complete Sprynter pilot — document problem, solution, impact with screenshots
- Package into 1-page case study + testimonial
- 1-2 more e-commerce pilots (discounted, not free)

### Week 5-8: Outbound Engine
- Facebook Groups: Egyptian business owner groups, value posts + WhatsApp link
- WhatsApp outreach: 10-15 personalized messages/day
- LinkedIn: 2-3x/week AI use cases for Egyptian businesses
- Cold approach with free demo offer (15 min, no commitment)

### Week 9-12: Scale What Works
- Double down on best-performing channel
- Referral incentive: 10% discount or 1 free month
- Portfolio page with real case studies
- Partner with digital marketing agencies (white-label)
- Cairo networking (RiseUp, AmCham mixers)

### Own Customer Acquisition System
- FB/IG ads -> landing page -> WhatsApp qualifier bot
- LinkedIn content engine (AI drafts, manually refined)
- Automated follow-up (24h + 72h nudges)
- Lead scoring sheet

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Price-sensitive market, ghosting | Prototype-first, low entry prices |
| "AI" sounds scary/scammy | Lead with outcomes ("save 4h/day"), demo don't pitch |
| USD costs vs EGP revenue | Free tiers, self-host, build margins at 50+ EGP/USD |
| Scope creep | Fixed-scope proposals, change requests = new quote |
| No hierarchy (3 founders) | Naturally specialize as workload grows |
| Freelancer competition | Ongoing partnership + Arabic-first + business understanding |
