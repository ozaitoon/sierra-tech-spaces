# Sierra Tech Spaces Customer Offer & Sales Script

## Core Promise

Sierra Tech Spaces helps businesses remove repetitive work, capture more leads, and respond faster to customers using practical AI systems built around their real pain points.

We do not start by selling a big project. We start by understanding where the business is losing time, money, or opportunities. Then we create a focused demo for free so the client can see exactly how the solution would work before making a decision.

**Simple message:** Tell us what slows your business down. We will map the problem, build a working demo, and show you what can be automated before you pay.

## Main Sales Angles

### Lead With Pain, Not Technology

Instead of saying:

> We build AI automations.

Say:

> We help you remove the repetitive work that wastes your team's time every day.

### Sell The Demo, Not The Project

Instead of asking them to commit immediately, say:

> Before we ask you to pay for anything, we will build a small demo around your actual business problem so you can see what the solution would look like.

### Focus On Outcomes

Use outcomes like:

- Save hours every week
- Respond to leads instantly
- Stop losing inquiries
- Reduce manual work
- Make customer service faster
- Track everything in one place
- Improve follow-up
- Give owners better visibility

### Make It Feel Low Risk

Key phrases:

- No commitment before the demo
- Built around your real workflow
- You only move forward if it makes sense
- We start with the fastest win
- If we cannot clearly help, we will say so

## Objection Handling

### "We are not ready for AI."

That is completely fine. We do not start with AI for the sake of AI. We start with one repetitive problem in your business. If automation can solve it, we show you. If not, we tell you.

### "Is this expensive?"

It depends on the scope, but we do not ask you to commit before seeing value. We first create a focused demo for free. If it solves a real problem, we scope the full version around the return it can create.

### "We already have staff doing this."

That is exactly why this can help. The goal is not to replace your team. The goal is to remove repetitive work so your team can focus on customers, sales, and decisions.

### "Will it work in Arabic?"

Yes. We build Arabic-first workflows, including Egyptian Arabic where needed, especially for WhatsApp, customer service, booking, and lead qualification.

### "What if our process is complicated?"

Most businesses feel that way. We do not need to automate everything at once. We start with one specific workflow, build a demo, and prove the value step by step.

### "Why should we trust you?"

Because we do not ask you to trust a pitch. We show you a working demo based on your actual business problem before you decide.
